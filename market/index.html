Govhoo.
Open-source Signals for Federal Markets
Enter a company name. Gemini identifies their capability space and generates a keyword set covering their products, competitors, and contract codes. Those terms are run against USASpending.gov — the U.S. government’s public database of federal contract obligations — to surface who’s buying in that category, which vendors are winning, how spend is concentrated, and what contracts are expiring. All dollar figures are obligated award amounts, not budgets or projections. Classified contracts, sub-awards under $50K, and IDIQ ceiling values without discrete award records are excluded. Data lags real-time by approximately 90 days.

Experimental. AI-generated keyword sets may miss or misclassify capability areas. Validate figures against USASpending.gov directly before committing resources or presenting to stakeholders.
