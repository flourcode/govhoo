Open-source Signals for Federal Markets
Enter a company name to map federal contract activity in their capability space. Results are drawn entirely from USASpending.gov — the U.S. government’s public database of federal contract obligations. Gemini builds a keyword set from the company’s capabilities and competitors, runs up to 30 parallel searches, deduplicates the results, and surfaces spend concentration, active vendors, buying agencies, and expiring contracts. Numbers reflect awarded obligations, not budgets or forecasts. Classified spend, sub-awards under $50K, and IDIQ contract ceilings without discrete award records are not visible here. Data lags approximately 90 days behind real-time.
