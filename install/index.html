Install Govhoo

Add Govhoo to your home screen for a fullscreen, app-like experience. No app store needed — it loads fresh from the web every time.

1

Open in Safari

Go to govhoo.com in Safari. This won't work from Chrome or other browsers on iPhone — only Safari supports Add to Home Screen.

2

Tap the Share button

Tap the share icon at the bottom of your screen (or top, on iPad).

3

Add to Home Screen

Scroll down in the share sheet and tap Add to Home Screen. You may need to scroll past the first row of app icons to find it.

4

Tap Add

Confirm the name (Govhoo) and tap Add. The app icon will appear on your home screen — tap it to launch fullscreen.

1

Open in Chrome

Go to govhoo.com in Chrome.

2

Tap the menu

Tap the three-dot menu ⋮ in the top-right corner of Chrome.

3

Install app

Tap Install app or Add to Home screen (wording varies by device).

4

Confirm

Tap Install. Govhoo will appear in your app drawer and home screen, launching fullscreen like a native app.

1

Open in any browser

Go to govhoo.com in Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox.

2

Bookmark it

Press Ctrl+D (Windows) or ⌘+D (Mac) to bookmark the page. Name it Govhoo and save it to your bookmarks bar for one-click access.

3

Pin the tab (optional)

Right-click the tab and choose Pin tab to keep Govhoo always open in a compact tab at the left of your browser. It'll persist across restarts.

What you get

Fullscreen experience — no browser bars, feels like a native app

One-tap launch — right from your home screen or dock

Always up to date — loads fresh content every time, no manual updates

No storage bloat — uses virtually no space on your device

Common questions

Will I get an old version if you update the site?

No. Govhoo loads fresh from the web every time you open it — you'll always have the latest version automatically.

Is this the same as downloading an app?

It's similar in feel but lighter. There's no app store download, no large install, and no permissions to grant. It's just a shortcut that opens Govhoo in fullscreen mode.

How do I uninstall it?

Same as any app — long-press the icon and delete it. On desktop, right-click and choose Uninstall.
