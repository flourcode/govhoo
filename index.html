How we calculate the numbers
Why totals can look high
Govhoo totals represent cumulative obligations to date. That means the actual dollars the government has placed on contract, not ceiling value and not a single year's budget.
Active contracts only
Govhoo filters for contracts that recorded a funding action in the past 12 months, then shows the obligated value of those contracts. This reflects the real footprint of active work in the market.
Built for clarity
Govhoo is not a granular opportunity tracker. It turns contract data into visuals that help teams quickly understand a market. In a QBR you can pull up a search and everyone in the room sees the same picture.
