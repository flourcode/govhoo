Understanding the numbers
Why totals look high
Govhoo's market totals might seem surprisingly high. The totals represent cumulative obligations to date, which is the actual money the government has put on contract, not the ceiling value or a single year's budget.
Active contracts only
Govhoo filters for contracts that have recorded funding actions in the past 12 months and then shows the full lifetime obligated value of those contracts so you can see the real footprint of the market.
Built for decision speed
Govhoo isn’t a granular opportunity tracker. It’s a way to turn contract data into visuals that help teams quickly understand a market. In a QBR you can pull up a market and everyone in the room sees the same picture.
